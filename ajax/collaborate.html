Collaborate: Do you have suggestions for public-domain or Creative Commons texts of 100 pages or less that you’d like to see reprinted in a free edition? Would you be willing to write an introduction? Design a cover? Edit a collection? Sponsor a book of a particular author? In the future, we’d be particularly interested in expanding into poetry, Spanish-language, books by women of color, queer and non-binary folks, and publish contemporary writing!

Send a message with the subject “Crazy little thing called love” to info@citapress.org if you’d be interested in donating, or collaborating on producing new editions.

This project is a collaborative effort of love made possible by the generosity of our contributors.
